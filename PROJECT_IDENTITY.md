# Project Identity

## Single-Line Objective

**Organize your life automatically from your WhatsApp conversations.**

---

## What This Project Really Is

Not a task manager. Not a productivity tool. Not a CRM.

This is a **life organizing layer** that sits invisibly on top of WhatsApp. It takes the chaos of daily conversations — group chats, work threads, personal DMs, random voice notes — and surfaces what actually matters: the commitments you made, the decisions you took, the things you need to do.

No data entry. No setup. No discipline required. It just works.

---

## Core Belief

Your life is already in your WhatsApp. The problem isn't that you're disorganized — it's that the organization is buried in 50,000 messages across 15 group chats. The AI reads them all so you don't have to. It extracts tasks, tracks their progress, and builds a knowledge base of every decision and blocker — all without you lifting a finger.

The result: clarity, not complexity. A dashboard that shows you exactly what's on your plate, what's urgent, and what's happening next.

---

## Design Ethos

- **Zero effort.** The user never creates a task. Tasks discover themselves.
- **Invisible intelligence.** The AI works in the background. The user just sees a clean list.
- **Frictionless.** One click to complete. One click to deep-dive. No forms, no fields.
- **Personal, not corporate.** No teams, no permissions, no workflows. Just your life.

---

## What It Is Not

- Not a project management tool (no Gantt charts, no sprints, no burndown)
- Not a CRM (no pipelines, no deals, no stages)
- Not a notes app (the notes are already in your chats)
- Not a habit tracker (just tasks that naturally emerge from conversations)
